Everything you need to know

I am completely non-techy. Can I really take this course?

Yes, absolutely. You do not need a background in computer science or programming to succeed here. If you know how to use a web browser and can follow simple step-by-step instructions, I will teach you the rest. We use AI to do the heavy lifting of writing the complex code.

How are the classes delivered?

This program is a hybrid model designed for flexibility. You will get high-quality, pre-recorded lessons that you can watch at your own pace inside your learning account. In addition to that, we will have 4 live weekend classes via Zoom where we build together, review progress, and tackle complex setups.

What if I miss a live class?

Don't worry, all live sessions are recorded and instantly made available inside your learning management system, so you can catch up anytime.

How long do I have access to the course materials?

You get full access to all pre-recorded lessons, live class recordings, and course materials for one full year after the program ends. You can always come back, log into your account, and review the blueprint whenever you are building your next big project.

What happens right after I pay or start my installment plan?

The moment you complete your payment or start your installment plan, your secure learning account is instantly created and accessible. However, to ensure no one is left behind, the actual course videos and materials will remain locked until our official start date on May 30th.

What can I do with this skill if I don't want a regular job?

Becoming a Digital Builder gives you incredible freedom. You can start a highly profitable side hustle building custom apps for local businesses (like inventory trackers for supermarkets or booking systems for salons). You can launch your own tech startup without paying an agency millions of Naira, or you can work as a highly-paid freelance consultant from anywhere in the world.

What if I get stuck or have questions during the course?

You are never building alone. You can ask questions directly during our live Zoom classes, submit support questions privately right inside your learning dashboard, or post on our active student discussion board where I and your fellow builders will be there to help you out.

What kind of computer or laptop do I need?

You do not need an expensive, high-end "gaming" or "developer" laptop. Any standard Mac or Windows PC from the last few years will work perfectly. As long as you have a stable internet connection and a modern web browser, you have everything you need to build.

Is the course accessible for hearing-impaired students?

Absolutely. Every pre-recorded video comes with clear captions. Additionally, you can request full text transcripts of the lessons to ensure you have a comfortable and complete learning experience.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes! Once you complete the 4-week program, you will receive a verified certificate of completion. It is a great asset to showcase your new capabilities to potential clients, partners, or employers.
