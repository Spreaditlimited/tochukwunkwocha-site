How We Do It

A one-week plan made for kids.

We move fast, but we keep it incredibly simple. Your child can always pause the videos, rewind, and ask questions during our live calls.

Day 1 • Live Video Call

Meeting their AI Assistant

We kick things off together. We teach them how to talk to AI safely, give it clear instructions, and understand what it can (and cannot) do.

Day 2 • Recorded Video

The magic of websites

They will learn how websites actually work behind the scenes. Then, we use AI to build their very first simple webpage in minutes.

Day 3 • Recorded Video

Building their dream project

Now they turn their ideas into reality! Whether it's a page about their favorite hobby or a science project, they will guide AI to build it.

Day 4 • Recorded Video

Making it look cool & going live

We teach them how to ask AI for beautiful colors and layouts. Then, I'll show them how to put their website on the internet so they can share it!

Day 5 • Live Video Call

Fixing bugs and celebrating

We meet live again! We will fix any "bugs" they ran into, celebrate their finished websites, and talk about what they can build next.
