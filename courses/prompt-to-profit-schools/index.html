How We Do It

A one-week plan made for young creators.

We move fast, but we keep everything simple and fun. Kids can pause, replay, and ask questions during our live calls.

Day 1 • Recorded Video

Getting over the fear of AI

We kick things off together. Your child will learn what AI can do, how to give clear instructions, and we will build fun games and a real business website live in class so they see results immediately.

Day 2 • Recorded Video

The absolute basics of websites

They will learn the building blocks of websites in a super simple way. Then we use AI to help them build their first simple webpage.

Day 3 • Recorded Video

Building their first real project

Now ideas become real projects. They will use AI to build a useful webpage around something they love, like sports, music, books, or science.

Day 4 • Recorded Video

Making it look great and going live

We add colors, improve layout, and make their page look amazing on phone and laptop. Then we publish it online so they can proudly share it.

Day 5 • Recorded Video

Fixing mistakes and building what’s next

We fix any bugs, celebrate what they built, and show them the next exciting things they can create with AI.
