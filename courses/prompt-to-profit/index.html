Curriculum

A one-week intensive built for total beginners.

The course is designed to move fast without becoming confusing. The live sessions create momentum and clarity, while the recorded lessons allow students to pause, replay, and follow carefully.

Day 1 • Live Session

Orientation, confidence, and how to talk to AI properly

Students understand what the course is really about, stop feeling intimidated by AI, and learn the foundations of clear prompting for real work.

  • What this course covers
  • What AI can and cannot do
  • Why vague prompts fail
  • How to give AI a role, task, context, and constraints
  • Choosing a simple project for the week

End of day result: Students understand the path of the course and write their first improved prompts.

Day 2 • Recorded Training

Digital basics, simple web pages, and getting comfortable

Students become comfortable with the simple digital workflow needed for the course and begin building basic web pages with AI support.

  • Files, folders, saving, and organizing work
  • Browsers, downloads, and uploads
  • What HTML, CSS, and light JavaScript do
  • Building a first simple page with AI
  • Editing text and previewing the page

End of day result: Students create their first basic webpage and become more comfortable editing simple files.

Day 3 • Recorded Training

Prompting like a builder and creating useful small projects

Students learn how to think like beginner builders and use AI to plan and create small but useful websites and web tools.

  • From problem to project
  • Choosing realistic features
  • Better prompt structures for building
  • Creating a small useful tool
  • Revising AI output more intentionally

End of day result: Students complete a simple project brief and begin a useful small web project.

Day 4 • Recorded Training

Styling, light interactivity, Netlify hosting, and domain connection

Students improve the look of their project, add light interactivity where needed, and learn the simplest path to getting work online.

  • Making a site look cleaner
  • Improving spacing and readability
  • Using light JavaScript without fear
  • Uploading to Netlify
  • Buying and connecting a domain through Netlify

End of day result: Students get their project hosted or ready to host and understand the simplest publish workflow.

Day 5 • Live Session

Live build review, fixing mistakes, publishing, and the path forward

Students consolidate everything, see common mistakes corrected live, and finish with a usable process they can continue applying after the course.

  • Common beginner mistakes
  • How to fix poor prompts
  • How to fix messy layouts
  • How to ask AI for help when something breaks
  • Project review and next steps

End of day result: Students leave with a complete beginner workflow and a project published or ready to publish.
